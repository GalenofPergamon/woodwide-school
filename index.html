Welcome to Woodwide School

A collaborative community where learning is a shared journey

At Woodwide School, our mission is to cultivate a collaborative community where learning is a shared journey. We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where families who can afford tuition contribute to those who cannot, ensuring that education is accessible to all. Through integrated curricula and mentorship, we aim to enhance comprehension, retention, confidence, and social-emotional skills. By combining personalized and small-group learning, we nurture individualized growth and collaborative problem-solving skills, preparing our students for meaningful and sustainable careers.

What Makes Woodwide School Unique

Interdisciplinary, Project-Based Learning

Integrated humanities and STEM, grounded in real-world themes and Colorado Academic Standards.

Peer-Teaching as Assessment

Students demonstrate mastery by teaching peers, solidifying their own learning and building leadership skills.

Mentorship Model

Regular mentor check-ins and advisory groups support academic progress, social-emotional development, and goal-setting.

Small Cohorts

Personalized and small-group instruction fosters strong relationships and individualized growth.

Community-Supported Tuition

Families who can afford tuition directly subsidize those who cannot; families receiving aid contribute in other ways (time, skills, service).

Learner-Driven & Collaborative

Students shape what and how they learn, cultivating agency, creativity, and resilience.

Equity & Inclusion

Access is built into the model so that all students, regardless of financial background, can participate fully.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a transformative educational experience where students, educators, and families work together as a supportive and dynamic community. We envision a school where every learner is empowered to explore their passions and reach their full potential through a curriculum that seamlessly integrates diverse subjects.

By prioritizing learner-driven education and peer collaboration, we aim to develop confident, empathetic, and resilient individuals. Our graduates will leave us not only with the knowledge and skills necessary for academic and career success but also with a deep understanding of their role in creating a more equitable and sustainable world.

Our holistic, integrated curricula interweaves humanities and STEM, reflecting the interconnected nature of these disciplines. We empower students to take ownership of their education, driving what and how they learn and defining their own measures of success.
